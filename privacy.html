Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BabyDaily collects, uses, and protects your information. We comply fully with Apple App Store and Google Play Store privacy guidelines.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you sign in using Google or Apple, we receive basic profile info (like your name and email) to create your account.
  • Baby Profile Data: The name and birth date you provide for your baby to set up their profile.
  • App Activity: The actual logs you create—sleep times, feedings, and diaper changes.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data on how the app is used, which helps us fix bugs and improve performance.

2. How We Use It

  • To sync your baby's logs across your devices and with invited partners.
  • To display daily summaries and trends within the app.
  • To provide necessary customer support.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We use strict database rules (Row Level Security) meaning your baby's logs are completely hidden from everyone except you and the partners you explicitly invite.

We do not sell your personal data. Ever.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted services to make the app work:

  • Authentication: Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In.
  • Backend & Database: Supabase.
  • Subscriptions: RevenueCat (for processing in-app purchases).

5. Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time entirely from within the app. Go to the Settings menu and tap "Delete Account". This will immediately and permanently remove your info from our databases.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about your privacy or data, email us at arefsn89@gmail.com.
